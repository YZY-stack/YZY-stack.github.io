Biography
I am currently a postgraduate student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen under the esteemed guidance of Prof. Baoyuan Wu. I am also honored to be in active cooperation with Dr. Yong Zhang at Tencent AILab. Prior to this, I gained experience in research and engineering at SCLBD, Baidu, and DJI. My research primarily focuses on AIGC detection, AI4Science, and AI Security.
